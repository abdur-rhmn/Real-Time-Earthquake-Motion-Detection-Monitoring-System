1. ADXL335 → Arduino Wiring (Critical)

Your code reads:

X = A0
Y = A1
Z = A2


So wire it exactly like this:

ADXL335 Pins → Arduino UNO
ADXL335 Pin	Connect To	Notes
VCC	3.3V	ADXL335 works on 3.3 is OK.
GND	GND	Must share ground with Arduino.
X	A0	Matches your code.
Y	A1	Matches your code.
Z	A2	Matches your code.
ST (Self-test)	Leave unconnected	Do NOT connect this.

📌 Do NOT use any resistor. The ADXL335 outputs analog voltage, not logic.

✅ 2. LCD I2C (0x27) → Arduino Wiring

Your code uses:

LiquidCrystal_I2C lcd(0x27, 16, 2);

LCD I2C Pins → Arduino UN0
LCD Pin	Connect To
GND	GND
VCC	5V
SDA	A4
SCL	A5

📌 If LCD doesn’t turn on → your module brightness potentiometer may be too low.
Turn the small blue knob until text appears.

✅ 3. Buzzer → Arduino Wiring

You used pin 2:

#define buzzer 2

Passive or Active Buzzer Wiring
Buzzer Pin	Arduino
+	D2
-	GND

📌 If your buzzer has no markings, the longer pin = +.

✅ 4. LED → Arduino Wiring

You used pin 3:

#define led 3

LED Wiring
LED Pin	Connect To
Long leg (Anode)	D3
Short leg (Cathode)	GND through a 220Ω resistor

📌 LED must have a resistor, or it will burn.
